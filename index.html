Have you used the Coordination Structural Integrity Suite before?

The suite is nine structural standards for coordination systems — tools for understanding what your organization's structure is actually doing, where it is missing, and what that means.

Two starting points. The Explore prompt orients you to your structural situation and routes you to the right instrument — use it if you are new or unsure. The Coordination Structural Integrity Suite Assessment takes you through a five-stage structured process and produces a gap map — use it when you are ready to formally assess.
